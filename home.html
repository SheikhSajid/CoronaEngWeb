CORONA ENGINEERING LTD
We are one of the leading electrical engineering companies in Bangladesh representing several global electrical engineering organizations. We provide complete solutions in the following fields:
  • Electrical and Automation Processes
  • Control Products
  • Low-Voltage, Medium Voltage and High Voltage Switchgear
  • Electrical Components such as Surge Protectors and Isolators
  • Transformers - Both dry and oil type of all capacities
  • Power Factor Corrector (PFI) Panels
  • Complete Bus-Bar Solutions
  • Automatic Voltage Regulator
  • Diesel Generating Sets with Automatic Transfer Switch and Synchronizing Panels
  • Motor Control Centre Panels
  • Industrial UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) Solutions
  • Complete BMS and SCADA Solutions

These products are of the highest quality, having been tested in world-renowned European Institutions and hold esteemed certificates such as ISO, ASTA and KEMA. Here at Corona we strive to ensure that you never have to settle for anything less than the very best.

Our products and solutions portfolio is further complemented by a wide range of after sales services in engineering, construction and maintenance. Round the clock support is always present to be dispatched at a moments notice to fix any problems our customers may have.

Corona Engineering Ltd has supplied 11KV and 33KV Sub-Stations and electrical systems to countless prestigious projects in Bangladesh. A small selection (with examples) have been in the fields of:
  • Airports (Several TTA SubStations for Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport,Dhaka, Shah Amanat Airport, Chittagong, Osmani Airport, Sylhet)
  • Mega Shopping Complexes (Bashundhara City Shopping Mall, Japan Garden City Project and Tokyo Square Shopping Mall Project)
  • Office Complexes
  • Residential Apartments
  • Industrial Complexes in Textiles, Ceramics, Glass Processing Plants, Chemicals, Pulp & Papers, Cement Plant, Petrochemicals, Gas Processing Plants, Steel Plants, Hospitals, Pharmaceuticals, Hotels, Power Plants, etc.

For a more complete list of our capabilities and our completed projects please refer to our Company Catalogue
